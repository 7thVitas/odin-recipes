Chicken curry

Description

Chicken curry from the Indian subcontinent typically features chicken stewed in a tomato-based sauce seasoned with aromatic spices. This recipe, like many others, calls for curry powder (a spice blend made with coriander, turmeric, cumin, and chili powder).

Ingredients
  • 3 tbs olive oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 tbs curry powder
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tbs tomato paste
  • 250ml coconut milk
Steps
  1. Get a pan on medium heat
  2. Add olive oil, onion, garlic to the pan and lightly fry
  3. Add curry powder to the pan and lightly toast
  4. Add chicken to the pan and fry well
  5. Add tomato paste to the pan and stir everything
  6. Add the coconut milk, stir, bring the curry to the boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes
  7. Check seasoning and enjoy the meal
