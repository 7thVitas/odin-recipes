Ingredients
  • 1 medium size onion
  • 1 medium size carrot
  • 1 stick celery
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 1 can whole tomatoes
  • 250ml tomato pasata
Steps
  1. Get a pan or dish on medium heat
  2. Add olive oil, onion, carrot and celery to pan and fry. Stir to stop from sticking.
  3. Add ground beef to pan and fry until moisture has evaporated from pan.
  4. After searing the beef, add whole tomatoes and pasata to pann. Bring to boil and simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Season to taste.
